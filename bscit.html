BSc IT stands for Bachelor of Science in Information Technology. BSc Information Technology is an undergraduate science course of 3 years dealing with software, databases, and networking designed to impart theoretical and practical knowledge on storing, processing and managing information securely. BSc IT Eligibility for BSc Admission requires successful completion of 10+2 or equivalent examinations from a recognized board. Admission is mostly done on the basis of merit. However, there are few BSc IT Colleges in India that take admissions on the basis of entrance exams such as NEST, IISER etc. BSc IT course fees range between INR 90,000 to INR 4.3 Lakhs.
